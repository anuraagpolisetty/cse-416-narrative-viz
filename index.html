How Happy is the World?

The Gallup World Poll recently concluded a landmark study surveying the state of global happiness. Countries and governments around the world have been increasingly using happiness indicators to inform policy-making decisions and ascertain the overall well-being of the population. Experts in fields such as public health, economics, psychology, and statistics have described how measurements of happiness can be used to assess worldwide progress based on individual factors.

In this interactive slideshow, I will try to answer the question "How happy is the world?" based on factors such as happiness by country, happiness over time, and happiness correlated with GDP and life expectancy.
